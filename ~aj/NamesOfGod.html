Names of God

Blessings and praises and happy sabbath day
Here's a count to recount in this journey on The Way
We're the ones, the wayward ones, He came for and equipped
To walk away, and ye to run, from sin and from Egypt

Reflect each day, one by one, to 50 as we go
We do this, He taught us . . . and by it, come to know
That Our Father's nation, became in sin a wayward wasteland roamer
An example-to-us, to-retain . . . reflect, and count the Omer.

There are many ways to do this, and for comfort here is one
Strike each day with a name, of The Father, and The Son
Names of power, and of peace, names for profit and for trial
Names of God, to weigh on The Way, considering all the while

Here's how it's done, I'll show you some, His names to get us started
Names of the One who by His Son, wide the waters parted
Adonai, El-Shaddai, King, and Elohim
Son-of-the Most High, JEHOVAH—YAHWEH, Teacher, Nazarene

Only a-few names in, yet stirring within
Our hearts is strength by voicing
The name of Him who is-like med-i-cine
And cause for great rejoicing

Man of sorrows, Mighty One, Messiah, and Omega
Alpha, Leader, Lord of Lords, Lion of the Tribe of Judah

Rabbi, Prophet, Prince of Peace, Redeemer, Purifier
Resurrection, Righteous One, Rock, Refiners-Fire

Oh but, when we get in a rut, with faith in the foe-names we hiss
And as words give them power and we start to cower it sounds something like this
Anakin giants lie ahead and Pharaoh breaths hot at our back
And worse than this heat, is our fear of defeat, it's faith we typically lack

Now this is the chance, the circumstance, exactly the type of hour
To get up from the dust and speak in trust the name of our High Tower
So let's gather our stammer and strike with His banner a Mighty name of The Lord
Deliverer is chosen as we rush the heaps frozen, and dry, the Red-Sea we ford

Dear God He saves us from our foes, and even from ourselves
Him none can oppose, so cast Him your woes, He'll crush all the various hells

He's Our Peace and our Potter He's Living Water,
He's Source and Shield and Light
Like a Lamb to the slaughter was He the silent Author,
And Finisher, we wield in our fight

As we think on these names, and trod through the pains, our road with hope we pave
Because His every title, for our recital, can turn a timid heart brave
Let's see an example the choices are ample, let's strike at the giants of strife
With the names of Him on whom we depend The Way, The Truth, The Life

Is affliction what ails, or some some mean thing that threatens your health?
Does sickness assail to no doctors avail and gobble your money and wealth?
Consider she, who was made to be free, 12 years her bank and body bled
Then came she, Yahweh-Rapha to see, to The Lord our Healer she tread
As the crowd pressed in, she touched only His hem, as she reached for the King of Kings
So If your blood rushes thin, stumble to Him, and call on the health in His wings

Ah but some may say, sick people die every day and where was the healing for them
Good people they, who would pray every day, and kneeling said send The Amen
And when children are some for whom death beats the drum, what name possibly consoles
Here's one for the parent whose heart is despair-rent: Daddy and Shepherd of our Souls
Yes not even death defeats the giver of breath so take heart you fathers and mothers
It's mean and it's sad but stand and be glad, not even death can beat up your Dad

So you see we are conquerers you and me, He knows our pain and sees us
No height or depth no-not-even Death keeps us from the love of God in Jesus

So don't let fear give you doubt? Love is the perfect cast-out
Strike at your dread and your terror,
To wipe out this stain, on Love is the name
To cast all your worry and care

Want treasure in heaven? He's your banker
Got storms in life, He's your Anchor
Is addiction your war? He's the Door
Choices wide, He's the Guide
Been betrayed, all alone, need a Rock? He's a Stone
Need a hand He's the Right
For your night He is Light
When you choose your preacher? He's the Teacher
For your hunger, it is said, He's the Bread
For your wine He's the Vine
For every blessing and every bother turn to God He's your Father

So you see of Him we sing, He is all, He is everything
Sing and praise the Ancient of Days
And say amen to The beginning and The End,
He's called Gentle Whisper, and Our Passover Lamb
Lord of All, the Great I Am

—August Hunicke
